Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 March 2026

Data collection

Hestia collects a random device identifier with your filter settings (price range, cities, agencies, minimum size). If you enable notifications, Hestia saves a notification token. This is the bare minimum required to make the app work. I don't care which homes you view or not: Hestia doesn't use any analytics and doesn't collect any data that would be worth selling. I don't want your data so Hestia doesn't collect it.

Deletion

Contact me through hestia@flori.sh to have your data removed. However, since there is no personal data collected, it'll be a bit hard to identify which database row is yours to be removed. We'll see how it goes!
