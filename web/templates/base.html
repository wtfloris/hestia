FAQ

What is Hestia?
Hestia is a home-finding service that monitors websites and notifies you about new matches within your filters.

Hestia is free?
Yes. I built Hestia for myself and once we found a home, I thought it would be nice to share it with others!

How quick is Hestia?
Notifications should arrive within 10 minutes.

Can you add a filter for amount of rooms, square meters, or postal code?
In short: no, because it makes Hestia less reliable. Please see this comment for the full explanation, and feel free to discuss if you disagree!

Does this work if I want to buy a home?
Not yet, but who knows what I might build when I am looking to buy something myself!

I saw this listing on Pararius and I did not get a message from Hestia. Why?
Pararius does not list a house number for all homes, so Hestia cannot check if it has already seen the listing on another website. To avoid duplicates, those listings are skipped.

Can I thank you for building and sharing Hestia for free?
Yes! Click the euro icon below the settings panel. Thanks!
